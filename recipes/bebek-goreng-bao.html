Bebek Goreng Bao

Crispy Duck Bao is a delicious fusion dish that combines the classic Chinese dish of Peking duck with the Indonesian specialty of Bebek Goreng. The dish features tender and flavorful duck that is fried to a crispy perfection, and served in a fluffy bao bun with a variety of toppings and sauces. To make Crispy Duck Bao, the duck is first marinated with a blend of soy sauce, hoisin sauce, five-spice powder, and other savory seasonings. It is then deep-fried until crispy and golden brown, resulting in a mouth-watering combination of juicy meat and crispy skin. The crispy duck is then served in a soft and fluffy bao bun, along with a variety of toppings such as pickled cucumbers, scallions, and cilantro. The dish is finished with a drizzle of hoisin sauce or other complementary sauces, which adds an extra layer of flavor to the dish. Overall, Crispy Duck Bao is a delicious and innovative dish that combines the best of two culinary traditions. It is a perfect example of how different cuisines and cultures can come together to create something truly unique and delicious.

Ingredients

for 6 servings

  • 2-3 lbs. duck, cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Chinese five-spice powder
  • 1 tbsp. sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp. cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Bao buns, for serving
  • Pickled cucumbers, scallions, and cilantro, for topping
  • Hoisin sauce or other complementary sauces, for serving
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, Chinese five-spice powder, sesame oil, cornstarch, flour, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Add the duck pieces to the marinade and toss until the duck is evenly coated. Let the duck marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the vegetable oil in a deep-fryer or large pot to 375Â°F.
  4. Fry the duck in batches until crispy and golden brown, about 5-7 minutes per batch. Drain the fried duck on a paper towel-lined plate.
  5. To serve, steam the bao buns until soft and fluffy. Fill each bun with a few pieces of crispy duck, and top with pickled cucumbers, scallions, and cilantro. Drizzle hoisin sauce or other complementary sauces over the top.

Enjoy your delicious and flavorful Crispy Duck Bao!
